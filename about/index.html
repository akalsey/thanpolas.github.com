My last full-time job was at peopleperhour.com where I built a visitor tracking system that allowed the service to store arbitrary information for every visitor that came to the site. Enabling the administration to have a crystal clear picture of the lifetime of their users and detailed conversion rates. The system used Amazon's DynamoDB for which I had to develop special drivers to interface with.

I've cofounded three startups in the past. Eyersee.org (offline) was my first startup that I co-founded back in 2000, it was a "geocities" type of service for the IRC community. geowarp.com a service that enables users to save their favorite locations and access them from mobile and web. boothchat.com a photo chatting service.
